About Us

At ProDigy Infotech, we are passionate about shaping the next generation of tech leaders.

Founded with a mission to make quality technical education accessible, we provide hands-on internships, expert mentorship, and real-world projects to aspiring professionals.

Our core team consists of industry experts, educators, and developers who work together to bridge the gap between academics and industry expectations.

With headquarters in Mumbai, Maharashtra, we operate fully remotely and serve learners across India and beyond.

We believe in learning by doing — whether it's coding, designing, or cloud infrastructure — our programs focus on practical application and industry-ready skills.

Join us to become part of a dynamic community committed to innovation, collaboration, and continuous growth.
